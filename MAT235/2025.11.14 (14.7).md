### Example 6
If $u(x,t)=e^{at}\sin(bx)$ satisfies the heat equation $u_{t}=u_{xx}$
Find relationship between $a$ and $b$.

#### Solution
$$\begin{align}
u_{t}(x,t)&=ae^{at}\sin(bx)\\
u_{x}&= be^{at}\cos(bx)\\
u_{xx} &=be^{at}\cdot-\sin(bx)
\end{align}$$
Using graph equation:
$$\begin{align}
ae^{at}\sin(bx)&=-b^2e^{at}\sin(bx) \\
a&=-b^2
\end{align}$$
Check $a=-1$, $b=1$
Hence, $u(x,t)=e^{-t}\sin(bx)$
Solves the heat equation.
