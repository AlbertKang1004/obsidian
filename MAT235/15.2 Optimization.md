Contour of:
$$
f(x,y_{ })  = x^2+ 2y^2+x^{2}y+ 3$$
Verify, that $(2, -1)$, $(0, 0)$, $(-2, 1)$ are **critical points** of $f$.
What type are they?
For $(0,0)$:
- $f_{x}(0, 0) = 0$
- $\displaystyle\frac{\Delta f}{\Delta x} =  \frac{f(x_{2}, 0) - f(x_{1}, 0)}{x_{2}- x_{1}} = \frac{4-4}{x_{2}- x_{1}} = 0$
- $\displaystyle f_{y}(0,0) = \frac{f(0, y_{2}) - f(0, y_{1})}{y_{2} - y_{1}}=\frac{4-4}{y_{2}-y_{1}} = 0$
For saddle points $(2,-1), (-2, 1)$, they have both **increasing** and **decreasing** direction.
